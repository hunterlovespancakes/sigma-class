Sigma Class is a teacher-supervised study collaboration platform designed for safe academic communication.

1. Monitoring

  • All chat activity is monitored by school staff.
  • Messages may be logged for safety review.
  • Inappropriate behavior may result in removal.

2. Student Protection

  • No private messaging is allowed.
  • No anonymous usernames.
  • Only subject-based study rooms are available.
  • All messages are public within each study room.

3. Prohibited Behavior

  • Cyberbullying or harassment
  • Sharing personal information
  • Attempting to bypass filters
  • Non-academic use of the platform

4. Filtering Technology

  • Automatic word filtering is active.
  • Reported messages are reviewed by moderators.

5. Educational Purpose

  • This platform exists solely for academic collaboration.
  • It is not a social networking website.
